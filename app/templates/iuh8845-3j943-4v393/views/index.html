Famiy Caravan Holidays

Our caravan is set behind the moutain with stunning views of the countryside. Included in your stay is free private parking and unlimited use of park facilities. The caravan sleeps up to a maximum of 8 people.

Located on one of the best caravan parks, you will have no shortage of activities to partake in. Including swimming, bowling and fishing.

What Should I Bring?

The caravan is supplied with fresh duvets and pillows but the linen is not provided. We offer packs for hire or you can bring your own. The caravan is cleaned weekly to ensure your stay is pristine.
